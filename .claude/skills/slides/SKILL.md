# Presentation Design

Presentation style should resemble Apple Keynote.

Minimal.

Elegant.

Visual.

Professional.

---

## One Slide

One Message.

Never combine multiple ideas.

---

## Text

Maximum

40 words

per slide.

Replace paragraphs with visuals.

---

## Typography

Large title.

Small subtitle.

Generous whitespace.

---

## Visuals

Prefer

Diagrams

Flowcharts

Icons

Timelines

Architecture

Screenshots

Avoid long bullet lists.

---

## Presentation Flow

Problem

↓

Current Limitation

↓

Our Solution

↓

System Architecture

↓

AI Pipeline

↓

Dashboard

↓

Demo

↓

Expected Results

↓

Future Work

---

## Color

Dark background.

White text.

Blue accent.

Amber for AI.

Minimal gradients.

---

## Goal

Every slide should be understandable within 5 seconds.
